ABOUT US

Mzamomtsha Primary School was built in 1999, a public school located at Valangethambo Street. . Mzamomtsha Primary school is one of the Public primary schools in urban Driftsands suburb. It is a section 21 school with ABE functions and classified under quintile 3 as a no fee institution. In the year of 2014, 293 learners were registered, including 51 Grade R leaners. In 2016, the school had 359 leaners served by 7 teachers with the student teacher’s ratio of 52:1. Mzamomtsha Primary School registered and prosperous School under the leadership of the experienced principal S.G Kuze, 9 talented teachers and precious learner.

This year marks my fourteenth year in education. I have varied experience as a teacher and school leader. For the first seven years of my career I served at both the elementary and middle school levels, teaching a variety of grades and content areas. I then served for two years as an assistant principal at two middle schools, and for the last four years as a principal, one year in Port Elizabeth, and three years in Mossel Bay, Western Cape.

Headmaster

My name is Simphiwe Kuze, and it is a privilege and an honor to be joining the Mzamomtsha Primary School team. My family, (wife, Babalwa, son, Ovuyo, daughter, Ovayo) and I are Xhosa natives. We spent the last three years in Uitenhage, Port Elizabeth, which is in Eastern Cape, and are beyond excited to bein Cape Town. ... I have read and heard nothing but incredible information about the strength of the Mzamomtsha Primary School and community. Each time I interact with any member of the school, community, or district, the message has been the same, “You are very lucky. You landed in a beautiful place, and at an exceptional school.” Please know, I don’t take that fact lightly. My goal is to jump in, continue to learn about the community, and work tirelessly to lead our school in a positive direction.

I love serving as a principal! My passion is developing positive relationships with everyone within the community, working with students as they grow, supporting teachers and staff so that they can reach their greatest potential, strengthening a connection between school and home, and a fostering a positive school culture. At our core, we know that the school to home partnership is what allows us to provide an extraordinary school experience for all students. I look forward to meeting each of you and learning how I can best support you throughout this year, and many more to come.
